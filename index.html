I'm a 23 y/o self-taught artist who has been drawing for over 3 years now.
Over the years, drawing has advanced from my pastime activity to something that unfolded greater understanding and perception. I was amazed by how much I wasn't seeing when I was looking around. I'm far from perfect but drawing reminds me that I'm capable of change and growth. Rather than dwelling on the past and stressing about the future, drawing helps me slow down and allows me to anchor myself in the present moment.

pencil drawings, illustrations, posters, canvas paintings, and more..
